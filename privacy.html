Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

PingPulse ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by Mukul Mehra, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use PingPulse on macOS.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. PingPulse is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All diagnostics run entirely on your device and no data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Processed On-Device

PingPulse processes the following data locally on your Mac to provide network diagnostics:

  • Network path status: Whether your device is connected via Wi-Fi, Ethernet, or is offline.
  • TCP probe latency: Round-trip times to public DNS servers (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 and Google 8.8.8.8) to measure WAN connectivity.
  • DNS resolution latency: Time to resolve a standard domain (example.com) to measure DNS performance.
  • Derived metrics: Packet loss percentage, jitter, reliability score, and root cause classification computed from probe data.
  • Local network info: Interface type, local IP address, and active DNS target(s) â€” all queried locally via macOS system APIs.

Diagnostic data is maintained in a 5 MB rolling log buffer in memory while the app is running. Data is not persisted to disk unless you explicitly choose to export a diagnostics log or health report.

Data Storage

PingPulse stores your preferences (refresh interval, notification settings, probe target, quiet hours configuration, trial status) using macOS UserDefaults on your local device. No data is stored on external servers.

If you choose to export a diagnostics CSV, incident report CSV, or HTML health report, the file is saved to a location you select on your Mac. We have no access to these files.

Network Access

PingPulse requires outbound network access to perform diagnostic probes. The app connects only to:

  • 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare) on port 443 (TCP)
  • 8.8.8.8 (Google) on port 443 (TCP)
  • Standard DNS resolution for example.com

No personal information, device identifiers, IP addresses, or diagnostic results are transmitted during these probes. The app only measures round-trip connection time.

Third-Party Services

PingPulse does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting services. The only third-party interaction is with Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchase management, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

PingPulse may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when your network status changes. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device by macOS. No notification data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

PingPulse does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect information from any user of any age.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties because we do not collect any user information.

Your Rights

Since PingPulse does not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. All data remains on your device under your control. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. As we collect no personal data, these rights are inherently fulfilled.

For California residents, under the CCPA/CPRA, you have the right to know what personal information is collected. We confirm: we collect none.

App Sandbox

PingPulse runs within the macOS App Sandbox with the following entitlements:

  • Network Client: Required to perform TCP probes and DNS resolution for diagnostics.
  • User-Selected File Read/Write: Required only when you choose to export diagnostics logs or health reports.

The app does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, photos, Bluetooth, or any other sensitive system resources.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
